Product Certifications

Icon Sleep products come with the industry's most respected seals.

CertiPUR-US®

CertiPUR-US® is a certifying body administered by a nonprofit organization that certifies mattresses, home furnishings, upholstered furniture, crib mattresses, and accessory comfort products.

For mattresses, foams carrying the CertiPUR-US® seal has been carefully examined and scientifically tested to ensure it passes the program's strict guidelines and standards. During the evaluation, the contents of the foam are tested, along with other components. This program is the most comprehensive and widely recognized certification process for polyurethane foam.

In a nutshell, CertiPUR-US® certified foams are:

  • Made without ozone depleters

  • Made without PBDEs, TDCPP or TCEP ("Tris") flame retardants

  • Made without mercury, lead, and other heavy metals

  • Made without formaldehyde

  • Made without phthalates regulated by the Consumer Product Safety Commission

STANDARD 100 by OEKO-TEX®

It's one of the worlds most recognized labels for textiles tested for harmful substances. If a textile carries OEKO-TEX's STANDARD 100 seal, you can be certain that every component of this article from the threads used to the buttons and other accessories has been tested for harmful substances and that the textile is guaranteed harmless in human ecological terms.

All tests are governed by OEKO-TEX's criteria catalog that is updated at least once a year to accommodate new scientific knowledge about textiles and to meet statutory requirements.

All Icon Sleep mattresses carry these seals of quality that reflect consumer confidence and trust rating.
